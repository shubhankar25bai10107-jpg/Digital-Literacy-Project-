#Stay Safe Online – Preventive Checklist#
#General Safety
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
Never share personal information on unknown websites
Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
#Safe Browsing Practices
Do not click on suspicious links or emails
Always check the website URL before entering details
Avoid downloading files from unknown sources
Use secure websites (look for HTTPS)
#UPI & Financial Safety
Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
Do not scan unknown QR codes to receive money
Verify payment requests before approving them
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online transactions
#Social Media Safety
Keep your profile private and professional
Do not accept friend requests from unknown people
Think before posting personal content
Avoid sharing sensitive information publicly
#Reporting & Action
Immediately report cyber fraud at cybercrime.gov.in
Call the cybercrime helpline 1930 for urgent help
Inform your bank quickly in case of financial fraud
